Privacy Policy
Effective Date: June 9, 2025  ·  Last Updated: June 9, 2025

Phyre is a dating and social discovery app operated by ApeCode Studios, based in Illinois, United States. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Phyre, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your name, date of birth, email address, and password.
  • Profile Information: Photos, bio, gender, interests, ethnicity (optional), occupation, and other details you choose to share.
  • Location Data: Your approximate city or ZIP code, and optionally your precise GPS coordinates if you grant location permission. Location is used to show you and others nearby users.
  • Usage Data: Swipe activity, matches, messages, live stream interactions, Pulse posts, and in-app purchases.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, push notification token, and app version.
  • Communications: Messages sent between matched users within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Phyre account
  • Show you relevant profiles based on your location and preferences
  • Facilitate matches, messages, live streams, and Pulse posts
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions via RevenueCat
  • Send push notifications about matches, messages, and activity
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • Improve and maintain the app using anonymized analytics
  • Respond to your support requests and reports

3. Third-Party Services

Phyre uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database storage, file storage, and analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Agora: Real-time video and audio for live streaming features. Agora Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase and subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps / Places API: Location search and geocoding. Google Privacy Policy
  • Expo / React Native: App infrastructure and push notifications.

We do not sell your personal information to any third parties.

4. Location Data

Phyre uses your location to show you nearby users and to display your approximate distance to other users. You may choose to provide your location in one of the following ways:

  • Precise location: GPS coordinates collected with your explicit permission via your device's location services.
  • Approximate location: A city name or ZIP code you enter manually.

Your exact GPS coordinates are never shown to other users. Only your approximate city or distance (e.g. "5 miles away") is visible on your profile. You can update or remove your location at any time in your profile settings.

5. Age Requirement

Phyre is strictly for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, their account will be immediately terminated and their data deleted. If you believe a minor is using the app, please contact us at apecode888@gmail.com.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication tokens, and access controls to protect your information.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your profile, photos, messages, and associated data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time through your device settings
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at apecode888@gmail.com.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To make a request, contact us at apecode888@gmail.com.

10. Cookies and Tracking

Phyre is a mobile application and does not use browser cookies. We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized, aggregated usage data to improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify users through the app. Continued use of Phyre after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
